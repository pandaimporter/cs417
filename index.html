Project 1


The tutorial part of Project 1 is below. I don't like a straight line of planets, so I modified the orbits to not look so static.


For the creative part of this project, I first added a background and decided to pan the camera to give the animation a cool "moving effect." I also added many objects to the solar system, which include:
- a full asteroid belt
- vertical ring for uranus
- a ufo and a small comet moving kind of erratically
- axis tilts/verticality for jupiter and saturn
