Project 1- Solar System, 2/17/2022


The tutorial part of Project 1 is below. I modified the orbits for this one, and the background is gray due to when I did this project.



For the creative part of this project, I first added a background and decided to pan the camera to give the animation a cool "moving effect." I also added many objects to the solar system, which include:

- a full asteroid belt, which was made by iteratively copy-pasting objects in a group to preserve animation qualities.


- verticality and an axis tilt for Saturn, axis tilt for Jupiter
- a ufo and a small comet
