Project 4- Rube Goldberg Machine, 4/7/2022


For this project, I animated a rube goldberg machine heading to the casino. It has its usual ball and rectangular shape knock-overs, but also has a bit of winning involved!


I originally had a six-part storyboard, but had to cut several things in production because they were too tough to animate and I didn't know how to do it.
